THE COMMUNITY

Active Adult Living Done Right

Bonterra at Cross Creek Ranch is Fulshear's signature 55+ community — a gated, resort-style enclave built by Taylor Morrison inside the larger Cross Creek Ranch master-planned development. It brings together low-maintenance living, exceptional amenities, and the natural beauty of the Brazos River corridor in one of the fastest-growing areas in Texas.

Unlike many age-restricted communities, Bonterra residents have full access to Cross Creek Ranch's extensive trail system, lakes, and outdoor spaces — while also enjoying their own private Club at Bonterra with a 10,000 sq ft clubhouse, resort-style pool and spa, full-service gym, and a social calendar that gives you more to do than most people have time for.

Homes are single-story and designed for easy living — open floor plans, covered patios, and low-maintenance landscaping so residents can spend time doing what they actually enjoy.

NATURALIST'S NOTE

Bonterra at Cross Creek Ranch manages something unusual — a 55+ community with genuine green infrastructure. The grounds around The Club are immaculate, and the community connects to the Cross Creek Ranch trail system. Painted buntings winter in the brushy edges along the creek.

AMENITIES & LIFESTYLE

Why Bonterra Stands Out

The Club at Bonterra

Bonterra's private amenity center is the heart of the community. The 10,000 sq ft clubhouse anchors it all — resort-style pool and spa, full-service gym, tennis, pickleball and bocce ball courts, cards and billiards room, and a lifestyle director who keeps the calendar full year-round. With 80+ resident clubs to choose from, the harder question isn't what to do — it's what not to join.

Cross Creek Ranch Access

Bonterra residents enjoy full access to Cross Creek Ranch's broader amenities — miles of hike and bike trails, fishing lakes, kayaking, and scenic green spaces along the Brazos River. The outdoors is literally part of the address.

Taylor Morrison Quality

Known for thoughtful design, strong energy efficiency, and reliable build quality, Taylor Morrison's single-story floor plans at Bonterra are built specifically for how active adults actually want to live — open, accessible, and low-maintenance.

CHRISTA'S TAKE

Life in Bonterra

Fulshear's growth over the last decade has brought exceptional dining, shopping, and services within minutes of Bonterra's gates. FM 1093 and the Grand Parkway (TX-99) provide straightforward access to Katy, the Energy Corridor, and downtown Houston without the congestion of inner-loop living.

The community draws residents from across Houston and beyond — retirees downsizing from larger homes, empty-nesters ready for a new chapter, and out-of-state relocators drawn by Texas's favorable tax environment and quality of life. The social fabric is genuine: neighbors who actually know each other, clubs and activities that match what residents care about, and a pace that's active without being exhausting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bonterra age-restricted?
Yes — Bonterra at Cross Creek Ranch is a 55+ active adult community by Taylor Morrison. At least one resident per home must be 55 or older.
What amenities does Bonterra offer?
Bonterra features The Club at Bonterra — a 10,000 sq ft clubhouse with a resort-style pool and spa, full-service gym, tennis, pickleball and bocce ball courts, cards and billiards room, and a lifestyle director on staff. With 80+ resident clubs, there's always something going on. Residents also have full access to Cross Creek Ranch's trails, lakes, and outdoor amenities.
How does Bonterra compare to Heritage Grand?
Both are 55+ communities with resort amenities, but they're in different locations and districts. Heritage Grand is inside Cinco Ranch (Katy ISD area, gated Del Webb). Bonterra is inside Cross Creek Ranch (Katy ISD / Lamar CISD depending on section, Taylor Morrison). I'm happy to walk through the differences in detail — just reach out.
