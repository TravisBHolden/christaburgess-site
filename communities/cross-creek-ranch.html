Cross Creek Ranch

Fulshear's flagship master-planned community · Lamar CISD · $400K–$900K+

Fulshear's Premier Address

Cross Creek Ranch is one of the most awarded master-planned communities in the Houston area. Spanning 3,200 acres in Fulshear, it combines resort-caliber amenities with the open-space character that draws buyers to this fast-growing corridor west of Katy.

The community features an on-site elementary school, a resident lifestyle director, and a development team (Newland Communities) with a strong track record of long-term community investment. Multiple builders operate within the community across a range of price points.

Why Buyers Choose Cross Creek Ranch

Lamar CISD Schools

Lamar Consolidated ISD is one of the most respected school districts in Fort Bend County, with strong academics, athletics, and fine arts programs at every level.

Amenities

The Creek House amenity center features a lazy river, resort pool, fitness center, and event space. A second amenity center adds to the already robust offering for residents.

Growth Corridor

Fulshear is one of Texas's fastest-growing cities. Cross Creek Ranch sits at the center of that growth — which has historically supported strong property values and resale demand.
