THE COMMUNITY

Fulshear's Premier Address

Cross Creek Ranch is one of the most awarded master-planned communities in the Houston area. Spanning 3,200 acres in Fulshear, it combines resort-caliber amenities with the open-space character that draws buyers to this fast-growing corridor west of Katy.

The community features an on-site elementary school, a resident lifestyle director, and a development team (Johnson Development) with a strong track record of long-term community investment. Multiple builders operate within the community across a range of price points.

NATURALIST'S NOTE

Flewellen Creek runs through the heart of Cross Creek Ranch — a genuine greenbelt corridor with native trees and wildlife habitat woven into the community. On still mornings you'll hear red-shouldered hawks before you see them.

WHY CROSS CREEK RANCH

Why Buyers Choose Cross Creek Ranch

Katy ISD & Lamar CISD Schools

Cross Creek Ranch spans portions of both Katy ISD and Lamar Consolidated ISD — two of the most respected school districts in the greater Houston area. Your specific section determines your zoning. Always verify the address before making an offer.

Amenities

The Creek House amenity center features a lazy river, resort pool, fitness center, and event space. A second amenity center adds to the already robust offering for residents.

Growth Corridor

Fulshear is one of Texas's fastest-growing cities. Cross Creek Ranch sits at the center of that growth — which has historically supported strong property values and resale demand.

Frequently Asked Questions

What school district is Cross Creek Ranch in?
Cross Creek Ranch spans both Katy ISD and Lamar Consolidated ISD depending on which section you're in. This is an important distinction — always verify the school zoning for a specific address before making an offer.
How far is Cross Creek Ranch from Houston?
Approximately 35–40 miles from downtown Houston via I-10 West to FM 1093. Most residents plan for 45–60 minutes during peak commute hours.
Is Cross Creek Ranch good for families?
Yes — both Katy ISD and Lamar CISD have strong ratings, and the community is designed around outdoor recreation, resort-style amenities, and a strong sense of community.
