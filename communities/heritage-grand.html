Active Adult Living at Its Finest

Heritage Grand at Cinco Ranch is Katy's most established active adult community — a gated Del Webb development within Cinco Ranch designed specifically for residents 55 and older. It combines the security and community of a gated neighborhood with resort-quality amenities and a social calendar that keeps life full.

As a Certified Home Marketing Specialist (CHMS) with deep experience in the senior market, I understand the unique considerations that come with this kind of transition. Whether you're downsizing from a larger family home, relocating to be closer to family, or simply ready for a lower-maintenance lifestyle — I'll guide you through it with patience and honesty.

A Community Built for This Season of Life

Amenities

The Heritage Grand amenity center features a resort-style pool, fitness center, billiards room, and ballroom — plus tennis courts, bocce ball, and a robust schedule of activities and clubs.

Low Maintenance Living

Del Webb homes are designed for easy maintenance — single-story floor plans, manageable lot sizes, and HOA services that handle common area upkeep so you can focus on living.

Location

Nested within Cinco Ranch, Heritage Grand offers walkable access to retail, dining, and medical facilities — plus easy access to the Grand Parkway and Westpark Tollway.

My Approach to Senior Transitions

Downsizing is one of the most emotionally complex real estate transactions there is. I don't rush it. I don't pressure it. I help you think through the timeline, the logistics, and the financials — and I involve your family as much or as little as you'd like. When you're ready, we move. Until then, we plan.
