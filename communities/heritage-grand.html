Del Webb is no longer building new homes here, so all inventory is resale — which means you're getting mature landscaping, established community, and a proven HOA. Homes in the $350K–$450K range move quickly, especially updated single-story plans with covered patios. The key for buyers is understanding what the HOA covers (and what it costs). Monthly fees are higher than a typical neighborhood because they include exterior maintenance, landscaping, and full access to amenities.
