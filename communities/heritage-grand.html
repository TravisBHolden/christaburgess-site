THE COMMUNITY

Active Adult Living at Its Finest

Heritage Grand at Cinco Ranch is Katy's most established active adult community — a gated, age-restricted neighborhood within Cinco Ranch designed specifically for residents 55 and older. It combines the security and community of a gated neighborhood with resort-quality amenities and a social calendar that keeps life full.

As a Certified Home Marketing Specialist (CHMS) with deep experience in the senior market, I understand the unique considerations that come with this kind of transition. Whether you're downsizing from a larger family home, relocating to be closer to family, or simply ready for a lower-maintenance lifestyle — I'll guide you through it with patience and honesty.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Heritage Grand age-restricted?
Yes — Heritage Grand at Cinco Ranch is a 55+ Del Webb community. At least one resident per home must be 55 or older, and no permanent residents under 19 are permitted.
What amenities does Heritage Grand have?
Heritage Grand features a resort-style pool, tennis and pickleball courts, a full-service clubhouse, fitness center, and an extensive calendar of social clubs and organized activities.
Can I buy in Heritage Grand if I'm under 55?
Generally, no. Heritage Grand is an age-qualified community under the Housing for Older Persons Act. Review the HOA documents carefully and consult your agent before making an offer.
