Established sections (Cinco Ranch South, Cinco Ranch West) tend to offer more mature landscaping, larger trees, and slightly larger lots at competitive price points. Newer sections offer modern floor plans, energy-efficient construction, and builder warranties. Both hold value well. Inventory can be tight in the most popular villages, especially for updated homes in the $400K–$600K range.
