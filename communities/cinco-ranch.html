THE COMMUNITY

The Gold Standard of Katy Living

Cinco Ranch is one of the most recognized master-planned communities in the Houston area — and for good reason. Spanning over 8,100 acres in western Katy, it's been the benchmark for community living since its development began in 1991.

What sets Cinco Ranch apart isn't any single feature — it's the combination of amenities, schools, location, and community character that has made it a destination for families relocating to the Houston area for over three decades. And as a RE/MAX Cinco Ranch broker associate, I've lived and worked this market as long as almost anyone.

NATURALIST'S NOTE

Cinco Ranch's trail system runs under some of the best crepe myrtle canopy in west Houston — spectacular in late summer. The community also backs up to Barker Reservoir, which means open sky at the western edge and consistent bird activity along the water.

WHY CINCO RANCH

What Makes Cinco Ranch Different

Katy ISD Schools

Cinco Ranch feeds into some of Katy ISD's best campuses — consistently ranked among the top school districts in Texas. Proximity to school zones is one of the most common reasons families choose this community.

Amenities

The Cinco Ranch Swim & Tennis Club, Splash Zone waterpark, and numerous parks and recreation centers give residents resort-quality amenities without leaving the neighborhood.

Location

Positioned along the Grand Parkway (TX-99) and Westpark Tollway corridors, Cinco Ranch offers fast access to the Energy Corridor, Katy Mills, and downtown Houston.

Frequently Asked Questions

What school district is Cinco Ranch in?
Cinco Ranch is primarily served by Katy ISD — one of the highest-rated districts in Texas. Depending on your specific street, you may be zoned to Seven Lakes High School, Cinco Ranch High School, or Tompkins High School.
Is Cinco Ranch a good investment?
Cinco Ranch has held value exceptionally well over 25+ years. The combination of top-rated Katy ISD schools, resort-level amenities, and sustained demand from Houston-area buyers makes it one of the most resilient submarkets in greater Houston.
Are there HOA fees in Cinco Ranch?
Yes — HOA fees vary by section and neighborhood. Buyers should verify the specific section's fees and rules with their agent before making an offer.
