Grand Lakes

Established elegance in Katy · Willow Fork Country Club · Katy ISD

Golf Course Living in the Heart of Katy

Grand Lakes is one of Katy's most established and well-regarded communities — a mature, beautifully maintained neighborhood built around the Willow Fork Country Club golf course. It sits in the heart of western Katy, feeding into top Katy ISD schools and offering a neighborhood character that newer communities are still working to build.

Homes here tend to be larger — generous lot sizes, established trees, and architectural variety give Grand Lakes a feel that's harder to find in newer master-planned developments. For buyers who want proximity to everything Katy has to offer without the brand-new construction price tag, Grand Lakes consistently delivers strong value.

What Sets Grand Lakes Apart

Willow Fork Country Club

A private country club with an 18-hole championship golf course, tennis courts, resort-style pool, and dining. Membership is optional — the views are not.

Katy ISD Schools

Grand Lakes feeds into Katy ISD's highly regarded campuses — consistently among the top school districts in Texas for academics, athletics, and fine arts.

Established Character

Mature trees, established landscaping, and over 25 years of community investment give Grand Lakes a sense of permanence and character that newer developments simply can't replicate.
