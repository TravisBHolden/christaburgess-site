Big Growth. Small-Town Feel.

Fulshear has been one of the fastest-growing cities in Texas for years running — and once you spend time here, it's easy to understand why. Wide-open spaces, newer construction, top-ranked schools, and a community character that still feels grounded despite the rapid growth.

Located along FM 1093 west of Katy, Fulshear sits in Fort Bend County with easy access to the Westpark Tollway and Grand Parkway. Several master-planned communities call this corridor home — including Cross Creek Ranch, Jordan Ranch, Fulshear Lakes, and Pecan Ridge — each with its own character and builder mix.

Fulshear Communities

Cross Creek Ranch

Fulshear's flagship — 3,200 acres, resort amenities, on-site elementary, and Lamar CISD schools. One of the most awarded master-planned communities in greater Houston.

Jordan Ranch

Johnson Development's Fulshear entry — known for its community garden, The Shed amenity center with a lazy river, and diverse builder selection. Lamar CISD schools.

Learn More →

Fulshear Lakes

A newer community built around scenic lakes and waterways. Offers a range of builders and price points with easy access to the Westpark corridor.

Pecan Ridge

One of Fulshear's newest master-planned communities — large lots, a nature-forward design philosophy, and strong builder lineup in the $400K–$800K range.

Fulbrook

An established Fulshear community with larger lots, custom and semi-custom homes, and a quieter, more private feel than some of the larger master-plans nearby.

Fulbrook on Fulshear Creek

A sister community to Fulbrook situated along Fulshear Creek — wooded homesites, creek views, and a natural setting that's hard to find this close to Houston.

Del Webb at Cross Creek Ranch

A 55+ active adult community within Cross Creek Ranch — resort-style amenities, low-maintenance living, and the full Cross Creek Ranch lifestyle in a gated age-qualified setting.

Cross Creek West

The newest expansion of the Cross Creek Ranch master-plan — newer phases, fresh builder inventory, and the same acclaimed Lamar CISD schools and amenity access.

Weston Lakes

A gated country club community centered around Weston Lakes Golf & Country Club. Custom homes, mature trees, and a more traditional estate feel on the western edge of Fort Bend County.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Fulshear a good place to live?
Fulshear has been one of the fastest-growing cities in Texas for several years running. It offers newer construction, larger lots, and a more open feel than inner-ring suburbs — with strong school options in both Katy ISD and Lamar Consolidated ISD depending on the community.
What school districts serve Fulshear?
Fulshear sits at the boundary of two districts. Cross Creek Ranch and most newer communities are in Lamar Consolidated ISD. Some areas closer to Katy are in Katy ISD. Always verify the specific address before making an offer.
How far is Fulshear from Houston?
About 35–45 miles from downtown Houston, depending on the specific community. Most residents commute via I-10 West or the Westpark Tollway.
