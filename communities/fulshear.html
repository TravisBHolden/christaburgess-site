What was ranch land a decade ago is now home to some of the most sought-after master-planned communities in the Houston metro: Cross Creek Ranch, Jordan Ranch, Tamarron, and more. If you want new construction, modern floor plans, and resort-style amenities without giving up access to the city, Fulshear is your move. Located west of the Grand Parkway along FM 1093 and FM 1463, Fulshear offers something increasingly rare in the Houston suburbs: space. The lots are bigger, the sky is wider, and the pace is just a beat slower. But don't mistake that for remote — you're still 35-40 minutes from the Galleria, 20 minutes from the Energy Corridor, and connected to everything via the Grand Parkway. The City of Fulshear has managed growth thoughtfully, with a charming downtown district anchored by local restaurants and shops along FM 359. It's a small town that's becoming a big deal, and the real estate market reflects that trajectory. --- ## QUICK FACTS | | | |---|---| | **Location** | Fulshear, TX 77441 / 77494 | | **School Districts** | Lamar CISD and Katy ISD (varies by community) | | **Price Range** | ~$300K – $1.5M+ | | **Style** | Primarily new construction, master-planned | | **Growth** | One of the fastest-growing cities in Texas | --- ## KEY COMMUNITIES IN FULSHEAR ### Cross Creek Ranch The flagship. A massive master-planned community by Johnson Development with resort pools, a village center, fishing lakes, miles of trails, and top-rated Lamar CISD schools. Homes range from the $300s to over $1M. ### Jordan Ranch A newer Johnson Development community with a focus on outdoor living. Great amenity center, trails, and easy Grand Parkway access. Primarily Katy ISD. Mid-$300s to $700s. ### Tamarron Boutique community with larger lots and a more rural feel. Popular with buyers who want space and new construction without the mega-community vibe. ### Fulshear Run Affordable entry point into the Fulshear market. The community events calendar is packed — farmers markets, food truck nights, holiday celebrations. The vibe is young, active, and community-minded. For buyers coming from out of state, Fulshear often surprises people. They expect "far out" and find a thriving area with H-E-B, restaurants, medical offices, and everything you need within a 5-minute drive. The growth trajectory means property values have been strong, and new retail and commercial development follows the rooftops. --- ## WHAT TO EXPECT ON THE MARKET Fulshear is primarily a new construction market, which means you're often buying directly from builders — D.R. Horton, Perry Homes, Toll Brothers, Taylor Morrison, and others all have active communities here. Having a buyer's agent who understands builder contracts, incentives, and negotiation is critical. Builder contracts are not the same as resale contracts, and they're written to protect the builder first. Resale inventory is growing as the earliest communities mature.
