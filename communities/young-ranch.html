Where Growing Families Put Down Roots

Young Ranch is one of Katy's fastest-growing and most family-focused communities — a master-planned development that offers strong amenities, competitive price points, and Katy ISD schools without the premium of more established neighborhoods.

Located along FM 1463 and Cinco Ranch Boulevard, Young Ranch offers convenient access to Katy's retail and dining corridors, the Grand Parkway, and the broader Katy ISD school zone. Multiple builders operate here, giving buyers meaningful choices across floor plans, finishes, and price ranges.

Why Families Choose Young Ranch

Value

Young Ranch offers some of the best price-per-square-foot value in the Katy ISD footprint — making it a smart entry point for first-time buyers and growing families watching their budget.

Katy ISD Schools

Young Ranch feeds into Katy ISD — one of the top-rated school districts in Texas. For families, it's often the deciding factor, and Young Ranch delivers without the price premium of older neighborhoods.

Community Feel

An active HOA, regular community events, and well-maintained common areas give Young Ranch a neighborhood energy that resonates with families looking for more than just a house.
