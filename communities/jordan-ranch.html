Prairie Living, Resort Amenities

Jordan Ranch is one of Fulshear's most sought-after master-planned communities — 1,500 acres of wide-open Texas prairie developed by Johnson Development with the kind of resort-style amenities that make weekends feel like a getaway. The centerpiece is The Shed, a stunning amenity complex with a lazy river, resort pool, fitness center, event space, and more. The community also features a neighborhood garden and sits with easy access to I-10 — making it one of the more commuter-friendly options in the Fulshear corridor.

Located along FM 1093 near the FM 359 corridor in Fulshear, Jordan Ranch puts residents within easy reach of the Grand Parkway (SH 99) and Katy's growing retail and dining scene — while still maintaining the open, unhurried feel that draws people to this part of Fort Bend County. Multiple builders offer a wide range of floor plans, from attainable entry points to premium custom-feel finishes.

Why Buyers Choose Jordan Ranch

The Shed

Jordan Ranch's signature amenity center sets it apart. The lazy river alone is worth the drive out — but pair it with the resort pool, covered pavilion, and full fitness center and you've got a community built around how people actually want to live. The community garden is a nice bonus — and easy I-10 access makes the commute more manageable than you'd expect for a community this far west.

Lamar CISD

Jordan Ranch feeds into Lamar CISD — one of the fastest-improving and most highly regarded school districts in the Houston metro. Families moving to the Fulshear corridor consistently cite Lamar CISD as a primary driver in their decision.

Room to Breathe

Larger lots, wider streets, and genuine open space set Jordan Ranch apart from denser urban communities. If you want a Texas-sized yard and sky that goes on forever, this is the corridor for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Jordan Ranch different from other Fulshear communities?
Jordan Ranch is anchored by The Shed — a resort-style amenity center with a lazy river, pools, fitness facilities, and event space. The community also has a neighborhood garden and easy access to I-10. It has a distinctive open, prairie-inspired feel and is one of the larger master-planned communities in Fulshear.
What school district is Jordan Ranch in?
Jordan Ranch is in Lamar Consolidated ISD (LCISD).
Is Jordan Ranch still growing?
Yes — Jordan Ranch is an active master-plan with ongoing new construction phases. Buyers have options from multiple builders at various price points.
