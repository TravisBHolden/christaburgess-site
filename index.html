✦ On This Place

The greater Katy area sits on the edge of the Brazos watershed — a land shaped by flood plains, live oaks, and the slow patience of coastal prairie grasses. Painted buntings pass through every spring along the creek corridors. The live oaks that line the streets add to the charm and character of Cinco Ranch. Choosing a home here isn't just a financial decision — it's a decision about which landscape will shape your ordinary days.
