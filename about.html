I grew up in South Texas, studied horticulture at Texas A&M (Class of '89 — Gig 'em), spent a year in New Zealand after graduation, and eventually found my way to Katy. That was 23 years ago. I got my real estate license, joined RE/MAX Cinco Ranch, and built a practice around a simple idea: treat every client the way I'd want to be treated if I were the one making the biggest financial decision of my life. That means I don't disappear after showing you three houses. I don't hand you off to an assistant for the hard parts. I don't push you toward a decision that's good for my timeline but wrong for yours. What I *do* is listen, research, advise, and advocate — from our first conversation through closing and for years after. Most of my business comes from past clients and their referrals. That's not an accident. It's the result of doing this the right way, every single time. --- ## HOW I WORK ### The Principal Advisor Model When you hire me, you get *me*.
