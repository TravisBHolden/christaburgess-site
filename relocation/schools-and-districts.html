✦ Read This Before You Choose a Neighborhood

School district boundaries in the Katy-Fulshear area run street by street. Two homes on the same block can be in different districts. A few hundred feet of difference in address can mean different schools. Always verify the school boundary for the specific address at both katyisd.org and lcisd.org before going under contract. This is not a step to delegate to closing day. Do it before you make an offer.

The Standard-Bearer

Katy Independent School District

6301 S Stadium Ln, Katy, TX 77494 · (281) 396-6000 · katyisd.org

Katy ISD is one of the most recognized school districts in Texas and consistently performs among the top districts nationally. More than 90,000 students across approximately 80 campuses. The district has built a reputation not just on academic performance but on the full program: strong fine arts, competitive athletics, robust AP and dual-credit pathways, and well-maintained facilities across the system.

✦ Christa's Tip: Use the Boundary Tool First

Katy ISD has a boundary lookup tool at katyisd.org that allows you to enter a specific address and see exactly which elementary, middle, and high school it is assigned to. Use this tool for every property you're seriously considering — not after you've made an offer, but before. I have seen families fall in love with a home in what they believed was a Seven Lakes HS zone, only to discover it was a Tompkins HS zone. The tool takes 30 seconds. Use it every time.

Serving Fulshear

Lamar Consolidated ISD

3911 Ave I, Rosenberg, TX 77471 · (832) 223-0000 · lcisd.org

Lamar CISD serves the Fulshear, Richmond, and Rosenberg areas — including Cross Creek Ranch, Weston Lakes, and the FM 1093 corridor. The district has grown significantly as Fulshear has grown, adding campuses and expanding programs to meet the incoming population. Fulshear HS is the newest and most prominent of the district's campuses serving the master-planned community corridor.

Fulshear High School

Fulshear, TX

Newer campus serving Cross Creek Ranch and FM 1093 corridor communities. Growing academic and athletic programs.

Foster High School

Richmond, TX

Established Lamar CISD campus. Strong academic and extracurricular programming. Active community support.

George Ranch High School

Richmond, TX

Well-regarded campus with strong fine arts and athletics. Named for the historic George Ranch property.

Use the campus finder at lcisd.org to verify which campus a specific address is assigned to. As with Katy ISD, address-level verification is essential before going under contract.

Getting Enrolled

Enrollment Checklist

Texas public school enrollment requires documentation. Gather these before your move to avoid delays:

✦ Documents to Bring for Enrollment

  • Proof of residency: Utility bill or signed mortgage/lease agreement showing your Katy or Fulshear address
  • Immunization records: Texas-required vaccines per grade level (meningitis, Tdap, hepatitis B, etc.)
  • Previous school records: Transcripts, report cards, IEP documents if applicable
  • Birth certificate: Official copy for each child enrolling
  • Social Security number: May be requested; not required — Texas law prohibits denial of enrollment on this basis
  • International families: Translated academic records are helpful but enrollment cannot be denied while translation is pending

✦ International & ESL Families

All children in the United States are entitled to a free public school education regardless of immigration status — this is settled federal law (Plyler v. Doe, 1982). No school district can deny enrollment based on immigration status or citizenship.

ESL (English as a Second Language) programs are available at most Katy ISD and Lamar CISD campuses. Both districts have experience receiving international families and have developed intake procedures for non-English-speaking students. Families arriving with children already enrolled in IB curricula should specifically ask about international transcript equivalency at the high school level.
