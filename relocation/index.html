WHY KATY · FULSHEAR

A Place Worth Putting Down Roots

West Houston has become one of the most sought-after relocation destinations in the country — and for good reason. Top-ranked schools, master-planned communities with resort amenities, a cost of living well below comparable metros, and a landscape that still has open sky at the edges.

But "Katy" isn't one thing. It's Cinco Ranch and its 30 miles of trails. It's Fulshear's newer communities with ranchland views. It's Heritage Grand for active 55+ buyers and Bonterra at Cross Creek Ranch. Getting it right means understanding which community fits your life — not just your budget.

NATURALIST'S NOTE

The Katy Prairie is one of North America's great wintering grounds for migratory birds — over 250 species pass through. Live on the west side and you'll have painted buntings at your feeder from October through April. Choose the right neighborhood and the land itself becomes part of the life you're building.

INTERNATIONAL BUYERS

Buying from Abroad?

International buyers face additional steps that domestic buyers don't: ITIN applications, FIRPTA withholding, international wire transfer protocols, and school enrollment for children on non-immigrant visas. These are navigable — but only with an agent who knows the terrain.

CHRISTA'S TIP

Start the ITIN process early — it can take 6–8 weeks. Your real estate attorney can file simultaneously with your purchase contract. I'll connect you with the right people on day one.
